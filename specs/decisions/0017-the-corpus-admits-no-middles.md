# 0017 — the corpus admits no middles

- **Date:** 2026-07-07 · **Status:** accepted

## Context

A session-long theme, named by John (2026-07-07): designs kept accreting
states between states — the shallow lift, the own-path fixed point, the
residual settings list — and every ruling was the same cut back to the
direct thing. A retroactive sweep found the pattern encoded: five body
middles (a "no member home yet" residual, a roadmap heading, a fossilized
ratification rider, "cannot yet check", deepening-era placement language)
and ~29 code comments narrating "until X lands".

## Decision

Body text admits no middles: no state defined by its own transition — no
"yet", no "until", no residual, no roadmap. A fact that names its own
replacement belongs to the queue or the fork board, never the corpus. The
razor that found them binds forward:

- the design states as one plain sentence, with no exempting clause;
- two states, never three — a third state costs a case in every seam;
- the name is the spec: when a verb or noun diverges from its plain
  reading, the reading wins;
- a declared surface ships with its consumer, never ahead of it — the emit
  payload's admission rule, generalized to every column and field.

The five body middles recut in this ceremony; the code middles route to
the pipeline as derivable work or standing violations.

## Rejected

Roadmap sections as reader courtesy (the queue and fork board are the
roadmap; the corpus restating them is a second, unchecked home). "Declared
and inert" columns as forward-compatibility (a consumer-less surface is
untestable and unfalsifiable — admission-when-consumed already ruled this
for the seam). Grandfathering the existing middles (a rule the corpus
itself violates teaches the violation).

## Consequences

`spec-system.md` gains the **No middles** form rule; `pipeline.md`'s
residual settings dissolve into the root member's fields; `builtins.md`'s
expansion roadmap recuts to the coverage bar; `distribution.md` folds the
ratification rider flat and recuts the keystroke placement to the
represented/unrepresented binary; `intent.md`'s self-hosting drops "cannot
yet check"; the inert-column pattern and the legacy-key lock fallback
route to the pipeline via the inbox.
